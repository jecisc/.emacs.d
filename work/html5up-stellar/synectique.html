Inventive Analysis

Inventive Analysis, created by researchers of the French Institute for Research in Computer Science and Automation (INRIA).

  • Provides insight into your applications: maps, detection of duplicated and dead code, check of cyclomatic complexity, inter-component communication, links of functional points
  • Delivers browsers and dedicated visualisations in order to target specific problems.
  • Shows the impact of changes, their consequences for the application structure and the technical debts.
  • Tracks modifications, validates the quality of updates, checks internal rules, compliance with good practices and the relevance of tests.
  • Assesses the processes of your intended architectural migration.
